4 OLS regression: Predicting tabooness as a function of valence and word length

Word emotional valence significantly predicted tabooness (p<.001). The effect of word length was not significant (p=.08). Together, emotional valence and word length accounted for 19% of the variance in tabooness.

curse.mod <- lm(Taboo ~ Valence + Nlett, data=dat)
